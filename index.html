  • Biography

    I'm a hacker that does design and development. I consider myself a competent but not extravagant designer and a pragmatic but not dogmatic developer. I spend more of my time as a developer than a designer, but I approach my development and programming work through the eyes of a designer.

    Most of my experience is with the world wide web, but I have also dabbled with native applications, interactive installations, and even some custom circuitry. My degree is in Interactive Multimedia which at it's core is about understaning human interactions with technology.

    I'm fluent in HTML and CSS, know javascript pretty well, though it seems that can change on a day-to-day basis. I admit reluctant knowledge of php, but would prefer to spend most of my time writing python. I also have a solid grasp on build systems, preprocessors, codecs, shell scripts, and all the other tasks, tools, and applications that go with building stuff for people on computing devices.
